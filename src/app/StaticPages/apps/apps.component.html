Customer and Instructor Mobile Apps

Signal Health Group has two separate mobile apps developed to help you grow and manage your business easier.

Customer Mobile App

The Signal Health Group customer mobile app is available for Android and iOS. We built the solution to allow your customers to easily find, register, and check in for a class. They can book an appointment and purchase a product, pass, or service in seconds.

Instructor Mobile App

The Signal Health Group instructor mobile app is built to allow your instructor to check in customers for a class without needing to be behind the desk, book an appointment on the go, and update their schedule availability.
